Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find PepDose - Peptide Calculator and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete data from Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.

How do I calculate a dose?

Enter your peptide amount, reconstitution volume, and desired dose. PepDose calculates exactly how many units to draw on your syringe.

Can I track multiple peptides at once?

Yes! PepDose supports unlimited peptide blends, unlike other calculators that limit you to 3.

About PepDose - Peptide Calculator

PepDose is the complete peptide management app. Calculate reconstitution doses for any peptide blend, log your injections with timestamps, and track inventory to know when to reorder. Supports unlimited peptide blends and both mg/mcg units.
