Privacy Policy

PepDose2 - Peptide dosing calculator and injection tracker

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

PepDose2 is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your health and fitness data should remain yours.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All your peptide calculations, injection history, and site rotation data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage system. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

What We Don't Collect

  • No personal information
  • No health or fitness data
  • No injection history
  • No usage analytics
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers

Third-Party Services

PepDose2 uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple may collect data in accordance with their privacy policy when processing purchases. We do not receive any personal information from these transactions.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data may be stored in your personal iCloud account, protected by your Apple ID security.

Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

  • Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings within the app
  • Or simply delete the app from your device

Children's Privacy

PepDose2 is not intended for users under 17 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
