Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a dose?

Enter your peptide concentration, desired dose, and syringe size. PepDose2 will calculate exactly where to draw on your syringe for precise dosing.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find PepDose2 and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, go to Settings in the app and tap "Clear All Data", or simply delete the app from your device.

Is my injection data private?

Yes. All your injection history is stored locally on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your health data on our servers.
