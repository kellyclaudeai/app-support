Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous purchases linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find MuscleMatch and tap it
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete data from Settings > Apple ID > iCloud > Manage Storage.
